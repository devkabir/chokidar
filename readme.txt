Chokidar provides website owners with a comprehensive set of features to monitor and analyze website traffic, optimize website performance, and enhance website security. With top 10 visitor insights, including most visited pages, vulnerable users, login attempts, hackers, visitor devices, and bots, website owners can improve the user experience, tailor content to visitors' needs, and block potential security threats.

== Description =

WordPress is one of the most popular content management systems on the web. It's used by millions of people around the world to create and manage websites of all kinds. However, with great popularity comes great responsibility. WordPress websites are also some of the most targeted by hackers and malicious actors. Therefore, it's essential to monitor and analyze your website's traffic to detect any potential security threats. This is where Chokidar, comes in.

Chokidar, provides a comprehensive set of features to help website owners monitor and analyze their website traffic. In this article, we will discuss the benefits of each feature in detail.

## Top 10 Visited Pages with URL ##

Knowing which pages on your website are the most visited is essential for several reasons. Firstly, it helps you understand what content on your website is the most popular. This can help you tailor your content creation strategy to provide more of what your visitors are interested in. Secondly, it can help you identify any potential performance issues with your website. If a particular page is getting a lot of traffic, it's important to ensure that it's optimized for speed and performance.

Chokidar, provides a list of the top 10 most visited pages on your website, along with their URLs. This makes it easy for you to identify what content is the most popular and optimize it for performance.

## Top 10 Users That Can Be Victims of Brute Force Attack ##

Brute force attacks are one of the most common types of attacks on WordPress websites. In a brute force attack, hackers try to guess your username and password by repeatedly attempting to log in to your website. Therefore, it's essential to identify which users on your website are the most vulnerable to these types of attacks.

Chokidar, provides a list of the top 10 users that are most vulnerable to brute force attacks. This includes users with weak passwords or users who have not updated their passwords in a long time. By identifying these users, you can take steps to improve their security and reduce the risk of a successful attack.

## Top 10 Usernames That Are Facing Brute Force Attacks ##

Hackers often use automated tools to carry out brute force attacks on WordPress websites. These tools use a list of commonly used usernames to try and guess the correct one. Therefore, it's important to identify which usernames on your website are the most targeted by these types of attacks.

Chokidar, provides a list of the top 10 usernames that are facing brute force attacks. This allows you to take steps to improve the security of these accounts and reduce the risk of a successful attack.

## Latest 10 Hackers and Why They Flagged as Hackers ##

Hackers are constantly trying to gain unauthorized access to WordPress websites. Chokidar, provides a list of the latest 10 hackers that have attempted to access your website. This includes information about their IP address, location, and the reason they were flagged as a hacker.

By monitoring this list, you can identify any potential threats to your website and take steps to block these hackers from accessing your website in the future.

## Top 10 Login Attempts with Username and Password ##

Monitoring login attempts on your website is essential for detecting potential security threats. Chokidar, provides a list of the top 10 login attempts on your website, along with the username and password that was used.

This information can help you identify if someone is trying to gain unauthorized access to your website. By monitoring this list, you can take steps to block these login attempts and improve the security of your website.

## Latest 10 Visitor IP with Country ##

Knowing where your website visitors are coming from can help you tailor your content to their needs. Chokidar, provides a list of the latest 10 visitor IPs along with their country of origin.

This information can help you identify where your website visitors are coming from and optimize your content for their needs.

## Top 10 Devices Visitor ##

Knowing what devices your website visitors are using can help you optimize your website for different screen sizes and improve the user experience. Chokidar, provides a list of the top 10 devices used by website visitors.

This information can help you understand how your website is being accessed and optimize your website for different devices.

## Top 10 Bots Visiting Your Website ##

Bots are automated scripts that can visit your website and perform various tasks. Some bots are good, such as search engine crawlers, while others are malicious and can be a threat to your website's security. Chokidar, provides a list of the top 10 bots that are visiting your website.

This information can help you identify any potential threats to your website's security and take steps to block them from accessing your website in the future.

## Benefits of Chokidar, ##

Chokidar, provides a comprehensive set of features to help website owners monitor and analyze their website traffic. By using Chokidar,, you can:

== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP 7.4 or greater is recommended
* PHP Dom Extension is recommended

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option -- WordPress will handle the file transfer, and you won't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Chokidar, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click 'Add New.'

In the search field type 'Chokidar,' then click 'Search Plugins.' Once you've found us,  you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by! Click 'Install Now,' and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual installation =

Manual installation method requires downloading the Chokidar plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).
